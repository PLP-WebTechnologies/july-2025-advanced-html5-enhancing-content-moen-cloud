LISTS

lists are used to group a set of related items, presenting information in a structured and easy-to-read format. They help organize content, improve readability, and enhance the accessibility of web pages by semantically grouping related elements.

There are various types of lists

ORDERED LISTS

Used for lists where the order of items is important, such as steps in a procedure or a numbered sequence.

  1. LONDON
  2. NEW YORK
  3. BERLIN

UNORDERED LISTS

Used for lists where the order of items is not significant. By default, items in an unordered list are typically displayed with bullet points.

  • MIAMI
  • SANTORINI
  • CHICAGO
